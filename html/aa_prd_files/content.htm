Product Requirements Document



  1. Title

    Auto Advisor


  2. Author: David Stumpe


  3. Change History


  4. Overview

    This web application will have two separate POV’s. One will be the car owners. It will allow car owners to easily compare auto services to determine what’s right for them. Vehicle owners will be able to create accounts, add vehicles, and make requests for vehicle repairs. The second POV is for auto shops. Auto shops will be able to view and make bids on repair requests in their area. The idea is that different shops may have different capabilities or parts on hand that would make the repair easier.

  8. Personas

    • Vehicle owners with no maintenance knowledge. Likely don’t like to say no. Accepts what they’re told. Owns an older vehicle (otherwise they would likely take a new vehicle to the dealership’s mechanics.

  9. User Scenarios

    • Vehicle owner takes their vehicle to their favorite shop. The mechanic tells them about multiple problems, what it’s going to take to repair them, and the costs. The owner can’t believe it and decides they want a second opinion. Before, they would have to take the car to one or more additional mechanics. With this web application, they will be able to post a request online and give a description of what’s wrong, what the mechanic said, and how much it would cost.

    • Mechanics using the app will be able to go online and see requests in their area and bid on them. They will be able to see the vehicle, the description, and any other info provided by the vehicle owner. The mechanic will be able to message the vehicle owner to get any clarifications or other info they might need to make an accurate bid. Bids will only be visible to the vehicle owner to prevent mechanics from adjusting their big inaccurately based on others’ bids.

    • The vehicle owner will then be able to accept any bid they want and begin to arrange an appointment with the mechanic. The web app will give the owner appointment reminders. It can also be integrated with the mechanics scheduling system.


  10. User Stories/Features/Requirements

    • User will be able to create an account

    • User will be able to add, destroy, and edit vehicles.

    • User will be able to create repair requests.

    • User will be able to accept bids.

    • User will be able to message mechs who made a bid.

    • Mechs will be able to make bids.

    • Mechs will be able to message users with additional questions.
